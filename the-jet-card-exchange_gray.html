Common Risks & Our Protection

Counterparty & availability

Risk: Issuer distress or aircraft unavailability.
We do: Escrow, verified programs, and substitute‑operator options.

Blackouts & call‑outs

Risk: You can’t get lift when you actually need it.
We do: Publish peak calendars and guaranteed call‑outs before you buy.

Hidden surcharges

Risk: Fuel/CPI, de‑icing, taxi time, FBO changes.
We do: “True all‑in” calc shown pre‑purchase.

Transfer restrictions

Risk: Hours can’t be sold or assigned.
We do: Only list issuer‑verified transferable hours or use approved novation.
